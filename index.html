About Us

There are 565 existing wildlife sanctuaries in India covering an area of 122560.85 km2, which is 3.73% of the geographical area of the country (National Wildlife Database, May. 2022). Another 218 sanctuaries are proposed in the Protected Area Network Report covering an area of 16,829 km2. We at Wildlife Sanctuary Services provide easy database management services to the famous Sanctuary 1 in Uttarakhand and Sanctuary 2 in Assam with hollistic approach towards aiming to preserve their greenery and wildlife.
